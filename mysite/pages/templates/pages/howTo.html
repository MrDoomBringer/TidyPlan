Getting Started

Create an Account

To create an account, visit the register page.

Add your Courses

Click the "Add Course" button to create a new course. Click the edit icon to edit the course name. Click the delete to delete a course.

Add your Schedule

Click the add_circle button located on the top-right of the My Calendar. Click the edit icon to edit the task. Click the delete to delete a task.

View your Calendar

Click the "View Calendar" button for a calendar view of your schedule. You can filter by month, week, and day.

Edit your Tasks

Click the edit icon on the task you wish to edit. Enter your new task name and due date under the respective boxes.

Complete a Task

Click the check_circle_outline to complete a task and recieve points. You can view the total amount of points you have by checking the Account page.
